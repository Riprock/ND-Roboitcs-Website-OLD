Awards

Below are the awards and events our robots have currently won in the 2015/16 season.

January 9, 2016

At our third competition of the season, the Green Machine dominated all competition. Winning in both the final competition & robot skills, the Green Machine is the robot to beat. Th_ also preformed well, finishing in 9th place.

December 5, 2015

At our Second competition of the season, two of our three teams preformed exceptionally in their Second competition. The Green Machine, finished in first place and qualified for both the regional and national competition. Robotic Knights also preformed well, qualifying for the regional competition.

November 14, 2015

At our first competition of the season, two of our three teams preformed exceptionally in their first competition. The Green Machine, finished in first place and qualified for both the regional and national competition. th_ also preformed well, qualifying for the regional competition.
