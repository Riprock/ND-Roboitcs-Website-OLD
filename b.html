Green Machine

Our first robot, the Green Machine is built in a compact structure, allowing for quick maneuverability.

The Robot

The Green Machine is the premier robot. Professionally crafted, the Green Machine is our flagship bot. Currently, it has been on a streak of pure domination, winning all three of our competitions. With a top score of 315, beating this robot is no easy feat. At the moment, the Green Machine is extremely accurate, making nearly every shot it sends off to the net. With insane driving speeds and shooting capabilities, it is tough to reach the level of excellence this robot holds.

The Team

The Green Machine has been created by an amazing team. Headed up by lead designer Terry and programmer Evan, huge efforts have been put into making this robot a success. Alongside Terry & Evan, Ethan, Harry, & Josh have assisted in the creation of the robot.
